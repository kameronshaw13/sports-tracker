GameTracker v136 polish patch

Changes:
- Plays pitcher row/baseball shifted left so the pitcher name aligns with batter-result text.
- Live tab no longer shows the Current At-bat/Last At-bat kicker; it just shows the batting team.
- Live player labels changed from Batter/Pitcher to Hitting/Pitching.
- Live player names made slightly larger.
- Live current at-bat pitch results are left-aligned and horizontally scroll when there are too many pitches.
- Occupied bases in the live diamond use the yellow occupied-base color.
- End-of-half rows are hidden for the active live half-inning until that half-inning is over.
- In-play pitch label changed from "In-play ball" to "In-play".
- Pitch ball numbers in Live and Plays are larger and moved down for better centering.
